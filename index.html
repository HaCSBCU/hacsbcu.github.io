Who are we?

We are HaCS - the Hackathon and Computing Society at Birmingham City University. HaCS focuses on bringing together like-minded, technology enthusiastic people to combine knowledge and create a fantastic network of people. Whatever classes you study or faculty you belong to, there's always room for you here!
