Events

There's more to life than social media...

Code & Chill

Code & Chill is our flagship meetup and our most popular event (obviously)! Twice a week, the whole society gets together in the Students' Union pub for an evening of chilling, socialising, and just generally forgetting about the stresses of student life.

Hackathons

Hackathons are what our society lives for. There's a hackathon somewhere in the UK every weekend during term time, and you'll often find our menbers attending one. Hackathons fulfil the aims of our society; they encourage creativity and teamwork, while also helping to make you more employable.

Workshops

Don't get us wrong, the stuff we learn at uni is amazing. But, it doesn't always cover everything, and not all courses teach everything. We have some members that aren't computing students, so we run our own weekly Workshops to bridge the gaps in knowledge. Plus, it gives our members an excellent opportunity to share knowledge around.

Open Days

Attending a Birmingham City University Open Day? Why not come and say hi? We're usually in the Project Space on Level 4 of Millennium Point. Come and chat to us about student life, the society, hackathons, or anything else you might want to know. We'll be working on some society projects, so you can even try your hand at developing a product.

Freshers' Fair

Have you just enrolled at BCU? Welcome! If you've got an interest in Computing, we'd love for you to join us - it doesn't matter what you're here to study. Come and find us in the marquee in Eastside City Park. We may even have some swag to give away!
